Japan Post Bank: If you have a stay of 3 months or more, you can open an account. There are many branches and ATMs nationwide, making it easy to use.

Mitsubishi UFJ Bank: One of Japan's largest banks, with branches and ATMs nationwide. It is highly reliable and stable.

Mizuho Bank: A major bank with many branches and ATMs nationwide. Account opening is also possible through the internet.

Shinsei Bank: An internet bank that allows transactions through affiliated ATMs and online. It is easy for foreigners to open an account, and you can check if you can open an account on the official website in English.

Rakuten Bank: An internet bank operated by the Rakuten Group, with many affiliated ATMs with other banks and convenience stores, making it convenient to use. It is characterized by relatively high interest rates.
